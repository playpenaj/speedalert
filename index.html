HOW TO USE

```

🚀GETTING STARTED

📍
Mount your phone

Use a windshield or dashboard mount. Keep the screen visible while driving safely. Grant location permission when prompted.

▶
Press START RIDE

Confirm the dialog. GPS activates, the speedometer appears, and speed limit detection begins immediately.

🛑
End your ride

Tap END in the top bar or the END tile at bottom right. The trip is saved automatically. Auto-ends after 10 min stationary.

🎯SPEEDOMETER DISPLAY

🟢
Green arc = safe zone

The arc from 0 to the speed limit turns green. Your speed is safely within the limit.

🟠
Orange = over limit

Needle turns orange when you exceed the speed limit. A ⚡ alert icon appears. Reduce speed immediately.

🔴
Red = 20+ over limit

Critical zone. Pulsed beep sounds, ⚠ alert appears, and the alarm activates. You are significantly over the limit.

🔵
Speed limit badge (left)

The red-circle badge on the left of the dial shows the current road's speed limit — updates automatically as you change roads.

📷
Camera badge (right)

Appears when a speed camera is within 250m. Flashes to draw attention. Alarm sounds if you are over the limit near the camera.

🔔ALERT ICONS & SOUNDS

📷
Yellow 📷 — Speed Camera

Single long descending tone (~750ms). Plays immediately when you enter 250 m of a camera, then repeats every 5 seconds until you pass it.

🚔
Purple 🚔 — Police Radar

Three-note siren (A4→E5→C5). Detected from OSM police enforcement nodes. Repeats every 5 seconds while within range.

⚡
Orange ⚡ — Over Limit

Double square-wave burst. Fires once when you exceed the limit for 1.5 seconds. Does not repeat unless cleared.

⚠
Red ⚠ — 20+ Over Limit

Pulsed beep every 3 seconds (1–20 km/h over) or every 1 second (20+ km/h over). Sawtooth alarm warble runs continuously when 20+ over. All stop when speed drops below limit.

🌐BACKGROUND TRACKING

🔋
Screen-off GPS

SpeedSense uses a Service Worker + keep-alive ping to continue monitoring even when your screen turns off. GPS tracking, violation logging, and camera alerts all continue in the background.

🔔
Background notifications

Allow notifications when prompted. Speed limit violations and camera alerts will show as phone notifications even with the screen off.

⚠
iOS limitation

iOS Safari limits background execution. For best results on iPhone, keep the app in the foreground or install as a Home Screen app via Share → Add to Home Screen.

📷REPORT A CAMERA

📍
Tap "📷 Report Camera"

During any ride, tap the yellow "📷 Report Camera" button on the speedometer screen to save the exact GPS coordinates of a camera the app missed.

💾
Stored in IndexedDB

Your reported cameras are saved permanently in your browser's IndexedDB. They survive page refreshes and app restarts.

🔁
Active on next pass

The next time you drive within 250 m of a saved location, SpeedSense plays the camera chime — just like an OSM camera.

📋
Manage cameras

Tap MY CAMERAS on the home screen to view and delete your reported locations.

📊SPEED LIMITS (AUTO)

🗺
OpenStreetMap data

Speed limits are read from OSM road tags via Nominatim reverse geocoding every 7 seconds. Covers all mapped roads worldwide.

🌍
Country fallback

When OSM has no tag, SpeedSense uses a built-in database of 40+ country speed limits by road class (motorway, primary, residential, etc.).

📋TRIP HISTORY & SHARING

💾
Unlimited storage

All trips stored in IndexedDB (primary) with localStorage backup. Data persists through page refreshes and browser restarts. Export to JSON to back up across devices.

📤
Share a trip

Each trip can be shared as a self-contained HTML report page with map, speed graph, violations, and camera log — all embedded in one file.

🗺
Route map

Tap any trip to see the colour-coded route on an embedded OpenStreetMap. Blue = safe, orange = over limit, red = 20+ over.

⚙️TECHNOLOGY

Web GPS API
navigator.geolocation.watchPosition with high accuracy mode. Real m/s speed from device sensors.
Nominatim OSM
Free reverse geocoding from OpenStreetMap. Extracts road name, postcode, country, and maxspeed tags.
Overpass API
Queries OSM for speed cameras, enforcement nodes, and police positions within dynamic radius of your location.
Web Audio API
PCM buffer synthesis — no audio files needed. Heartbeat keeps iOS AudioContext alive during long drives.
Screen Wake Lock
navigator.wakeLock.request('screen') keeps display on. Falls back to looping video on iOS.
Service Worker
Background keep-alive ping. Push notifications for alerts when screen is off. Periodic GPS relay.
Leaflet.js
Interactive OSM maps in trip detail. Colour-coded polylines, violation and camera markers.
Canvas 2D
Speedometer arc, needle, live graph — all drawn at device pixel ratio for crisp retina rendering.
```
